The 2017-2018 Postseason

Bowl TitleOpponentsFinal Score against UGAResult of Game
SEC Championship#10 Auburn28-7Win
The Rose Bowl#3 Oklahoma54-48Win
National Championship#1 Alabama23-26Loss

Over the course of the year the Bulldogs remained at the #2 spot on most College football ranking, even though suffering a 40-17 loss to #10 SEC rival Auburn in week 8. The Dawgs redeemed themselves by stomping the Auburn Tigers in the SEC Championship 28-7, which allowed them to proceed into the newly adopted College Football Playoff Bracket with the top 4 teams in the country. Barely passing Oklahoma in the Rose Bowl, UGA had their eyes on their foes to the West. The Alabama was a nail-biting spectacle, unfortunately UGA suffered a last-minute score to lose the game 26-23 in Overtime. If this season was any indication of the future of this program in the next five years, we should expect a National Championship Trophy coming to Athens VERY soon.
